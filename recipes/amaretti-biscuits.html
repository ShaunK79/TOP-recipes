Nanna's Amaretti Biscuits

These are the quickest, easiest and most tastiest treats you can make yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Almond Meal/Ground Almond
  • 300g Castor Sugar
  • 5 x Egg Whites ( Room Temp )
  • 1/2 19ml bottle of almond essence
  • Icing Sugar for Dusting

Method:

  1. seperate 5 egg whites from the yolk and mix well in a bowl.
  2. once lots of bubbles form on top of the egg whites, add the almond essence and whisk gently.
  3. mix the almond flour and castor sugar together in a seperate bowl.
  4. add egg whites mix to dry mix and stir together.
  5. once all combined, use your hands to ensure its all incorporated.
  6. let the mix rest for an hour or overnight in the fridge.
  7. prepare a large baking tray and pre heat the oven to 180deg C.
  8. using a teaspoon, scoop up heaped amount, roll in palm of hand to form a ball.
  9. place ball in a bowl of icing sugar and coat.
  10. place ball onto tray leaving enough space between balls.
  11. flatten the balls slightly which will create circles.
  12. dust with more icing sugar and bake for 12-15mins.
